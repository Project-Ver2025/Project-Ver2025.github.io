Project Ver

As we springboard off the Ver One prototype, our team of six 5th year engineering students at the ANU aim to refine the existing Ver One design to create the next iteration; Ver Two. Our mission remains the same, to design and build a small, wearable device to assist the low vision community navigate their everyday.
